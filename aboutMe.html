About Me

Dancer, Pianist, Farmer

My name is Maklain Shepherd and I grew up in Lake Shore, Utah where I reside with my family. My parents own a poultry farm called Shepherd's Processed Eggs, and I had the privelege of working with them and learning life-shaping lessons like the value of hardwork and the importance of charity.

One of my greatest passions in life is ballroom dancing. I started dancing at eleven years old and trained with the BYU Youth Dancesport program which helped prepare me to one day be a member of the BYU Ballroom Touring Company. I have been able to travel the world with this prestigious group and share my love of dance with people from all walks of life.

I am also a devout disciple of Jesus Christ and member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. I had the opportunity to serve a full-time mission in Charlotte, North Carolina and São Paulo, Brazil which has impacted my life in profound ways. Apart from being bilingual, I learned about the Savior and His divine Atonement that helps us all find true joy in this life.
