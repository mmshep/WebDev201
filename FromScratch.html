American Smooth

I have had the opportunity to dance for almmost 12 years competing in multiple different styles of Ballroom. I have absolutely loved learning more about dance and gaining a passion for this incredible sport! Though I have had experience in three of the four ballroom dance styles, American Smooth is my favorite.

Like the name suggests, American Smooth was first developed here in the United States close to the turn of the century. It utilizes technical skills from both International Ballroom and Latin Dancing with the freedom of greater expression and novel movement. The dances performed in American Smooth are Waltz, Tango, Foxtrot, and Viennese Waltz. One of my favorites is the Viennese Waltz - here is a video of me dancing with my friends in an American Smooth Gala held in England last year!
